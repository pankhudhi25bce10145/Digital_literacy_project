RESPONSIBLE SOCIAL MEDIA USE AS A COLLEGE STUDENT

? Do’s
1. Think before you post — ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
2. Maintain privacy settings to protect your personal information.
3. Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
4. Use social media for learning, networking, and skill development.
5. Be respectful and positive in comments and interactions.

? Don’ts
1. Don’t share personal details like phone number, address, or passwords.
2. Don’t engage in cyberbullying or negative online behavior.
3. Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content.
4. Don’t believe or spread fake news without checking facts.
5. Don’t spend excessive time on social media affecting studies or health.
