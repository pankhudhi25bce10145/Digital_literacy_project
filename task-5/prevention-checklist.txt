Stay Safe Online – Student Checklist
1. Use Strong, Unique Passwords - Create passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
3. Think Before You Click -Avoid clicking suspicious links in emails, SMS, or WhatsApp messages.
4. Protect Personal Information-Don’t share Aadhaar, PAN, OTPs, or passwords online.
5.  Keep Devices Updated
6. Secure Public Wi-Fi Use
7. Verify Job & Internship Offers-Cross-check companies before applying or paying any “registration fee.”
8. UPI Safety Tip #1: Never Share OTP or Approve Unknown Requests ??
9. UPI Safety Tip #2: Double-Check Before Paying
Report Cyber Fraud Immediately ??
10. If you’re a victim of fraud, act fast: 
a. Report at National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in 
b. Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930.
11. Quick reporting increases chances of recovering lost money.
